About Our company

Arcotech Safety Electromechanical Works is a Dubai-based fire protection company, approved by Dubai Civil Defence. We specialize in the supply, installation, and maintenance of all types of fire protection systems. Our company is committed to providing engineering solutions tailored to our clients' specific requirements while ensuring compliance with statutory regulations and environmental considerations. We prioritize optimal resource efficiency and high-quality service. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering the best solutions for fire protection system needs and equipment requirements. We specialize in the installation of firefighting safety equipment, including fire alarms, fire extinguishers, sprinkler systems, and more. Our mission is to ensure the safety of our clients and their properties by offering top-quality products and services. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you stay protected in case of a fire emergency.
